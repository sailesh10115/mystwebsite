Why Myst Scrims?

Myst Scrims was founded with the main motive, catering to competitive players, who want to grind and get better. We also give our players up to $20 per win to keep them motivated Because of arena queue times, it can be difficult to grind it, thus our customs are a good substitute and better practice. It is good if you come to play our customs and grind points, but the only thing you should be concentrating on is making yourself better over time.

Frequently Asked Questions


Myst Scrims is the largest NA Fortnite Discord Network.
We provide some of the best scrims for practice, and we also host
ranked ladders that allow you to progress and rankup to play against better players. All of our games are also money games!

If you would like to contact staff, please join our Discord Server then go to #modmail-and-report and react to the message.

To appeal your ban from any Myst server, please make a ticket in the #ban-appeal channel of our hub server.

To apply for staff, go to the applications page and fill out the staff application. You must wait for staff applications come out. When they do, you will need to fill out a form, answering a series of questions. Once you have done so, the administrative team for the respective server will view your application. You will be notified within 2-3 days if you have been accepted. Please don't DM administrators asking about your application, else you will be rejected.

To play our games, make sure to join our Discord server and verify your Fortnite account. Once you have done so, you are set up to play!
